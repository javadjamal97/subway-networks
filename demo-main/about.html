About Us

Informations about our company and our philosophy

Subway Networks, founded in 2016 as an oil and gas product trading company, has since transformed into a dynamic conglomerate offering an array of services that cater to diverse industries. Our journey has taken us from our initial focus on trading to becoming a multifaceted group with expertise in instrumentation and control systems, cutting-edge information technology solutions, robust Operational Technology - Cybersecurity services, construction capabilities, and innovative interior design services.

As proud distributors of esteemed electrical and instrumentation brands such as Schneider, WAGO, Camellie Beur, Larson Electronics, Morse Starette, and more, our core strength lies in the expert installation and commissioning of Electrical and Instrumentation (E&I) systems.

With a growing presence in three prominent GCC countries - Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, and the UAE, we are committed to serving clients comprehensively across the oil and gas, industrial, and commercial sectors. Our dedication to delivering top-tier services and products aligns with the highest standards of safety, reliability, and efficiency.

At the heart of our accomplishments is our exceptional team, comprised of experienced and devoted professionals who are genuinely passionate about their work. Their commitment drives us to not only meet but consistently exceed our customers' expectations. We are relentless in our pursuit of innovation, continuously exploring new avenues to enhance our offerings, ensuring that our customers enjoy an unparalleled experience.

At Subway Networks, we combine expertise, commitment, and innovation to deliver excellence across a spectrum of industries. Join us on this transformative journey, and let us be your trusted partner for a brighter future.
