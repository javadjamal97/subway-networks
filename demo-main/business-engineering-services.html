ENGINEERING SERVICES

Empower Your Industry with Our Instrumentation and Control Engineering Expertise: Our engineering services division is your go-to partner for a comprehensive suite of services encompassing the selection, installation, testing, and commissioning of cutting-edge instruments and automation systems. As proud system integrators for Siemens automation systems, we excel in delivering services that cover system upgrades, meticulous maintenance, and troubleshooting across PLC, HMI, VFD, and SCADA systems. Our expertise further extends to customized solutions, involving design engineering,

wiring, programming, and graphics development for control panels. Our service portfolio includes:
• Instrument Selection and Procurement • Instrument Installation and Commissioning • Automation System Design and Integration • PLC Programming and Configuration • HMI Development and Integration • VFD Programming and Configuration • SCADA System Design and Implementation • Control Panel Design, Wiring, and Programming We cater to an array of industries, including but not limited to oil and gas, petrochemicals, power generation, manufacturing, and food and beverage. Our team of passionate and highly qualified engineers is dedicated to delivering the epitome of service excellence, ensuring your industry thrives with the best solutions available.
