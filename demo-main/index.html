Subway Networks, established in 2016 with a primary focus on oil and gas product trading. Since our inception, we have embarked on a transformative journey, evolving into a diversified conglomerate. Today, our multifaceted group of companies offers a wide spectrum of services encompassing engineering expertise in instrumentation and control systems, cutting-edge information technology solutions, robust Operational Technology - Cybersecurity services, construction capabilities, and innovative interior design services. And, we proudly serve as trusted distributors for renowned electrical and instrumentation brands, including Schneider, WAGO, Camellie Beur, Larson Electronics, & more.

Subway Networks has expanded its footprint across three prominent GCC countries: Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, and the UAE. Our overarching mission revolves around serving our clients comprehensively, catering to their distinct needs in the oil and gas, industrial, and commercial sectors. We are unwavering in our commitment to delivering services and products of the highest caliber, adhering rigorously to the stringent standards of safety, reliability, and efficiency.
